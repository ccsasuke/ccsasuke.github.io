# [Research](#research)
My research interests lie broadly in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I am particularly intrigued by the interplay between knowledge and language.
In recent years, I have been working on knowledge-intensive NLP problems such as Open-Domain Question Answering, Neural Retrieval, and more recently Retrieval-Augmented Language Models.

Previously, my PhD research was about learning deep representations for low-resource / zero-resource cross-lingual model transfer.
